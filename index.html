My Life Style

Jan 1, 2023

Victor Oti also known as Scoffi drummer is a Christian drummer, rendering service for God through his drum skills. And he was born into the family of Rev. & Mrs. Oti on the 13th of March. His parents are Anglican faithfuls likewise Victor. His father is one of the priest in the Anglican church in Benin City, Edo State. Victor is the last born and the only son of his parents, he has an elder sister named Amarachi Esther Oti.

My Hobby

Jan 2, 2024

Victor is opportuned to play drum at his local church (St. Paul's Anglican Church, Ologbo). Victor started drumming at church at the age of 13, because at that time some of the church drummers travelled out in search of greener pastures.

Victor Oti

Victor faced some difficulties and some challenges from some choristers and it was really challenging to him. They'll always complain about the way he played during Sunday's service, Victor didn't give up, instead he kept pushing on by observing his private drum practice. In some of his rehearsals, Victor will play along with some Gospel songs both worship and praise songs, this helped him improve in his drumming skills. If Victor had given up then, I wonder what he would have been doing now as a way of rendering his service to God as a talent.
